Design




What is Design?

The Subject Design Consists of 4 Criterias. Inquiry And Analyzing, Developing Ideas,
Creating Solution And Evaluation. Each Of These Criterias Lead To The Final Solution
And Shows The Entire Process. For Example When Solving An Issue We Analyse It Then Think
Of Existing Issues, Create Our Own Solution And The Last Thing Is Performing The Solution.



How will studing Design help in The Future?

In The Future For Example Your Job You Might Face Issues So You Can Use The Design Cycle To Solve It,
Or If You Are Becoming An Architect Then You Would Need To Make Houses For People That Have Certain
Needs Or Wants. You Would Need To See His Idea, His Old House, And Differences Needed. After All That
You Make The Building Which Would Be Evaluation.




What skills do you Develope in Design?

The Subject Design Helps Us Improve On Inquirer, Open Minded and critical thinking As We
Have To Think About The Problem And Inquire, Analyze, Make A Solution And Implement It.
